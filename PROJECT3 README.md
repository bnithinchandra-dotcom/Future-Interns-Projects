# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard evaluates marketing campaign effectiveness and customer conversion behavior. It provides insights into lead generation, conversion performance, and customer engagement.

## Objectives

- Analyze marketing funnel performance
- Measure conversion rates and drop-offs
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness
- Identify high-performing customer segments
- Improve marketing ROI

## Key Metrics

- Total Leads: 41K
- Total Conversions: 5K
- Conversion Rate: 11.27%
- Drop-Off Rate: 88.73%
- Total Non-Conversions: 37K
- Previously Contacted Customers: 6K

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Lead-to-Customer Funnel Analysis
- Conversion by Contact Channel
- Conversion by Job Category
- Conversion by Education Level
- Campaign Outcome Analysis

## Key Insights

- Only 11.27% of leads converted into customers.
- Cellular campaigns generated more conversions than telephone campaigns.
- Previous successful interactions increased conversion likelihood.
- Customer occupation influenced campaign effectiveness.
- Education level impacted conversion behavior.

## Recommendations

- Increase focus on cellular communication channels.
- Prioritize customers with successful campaign histories.
- Create targeted marketing strategies for high-performing segments.
- Implement lead nurturing programs to reduce drop-offs.
