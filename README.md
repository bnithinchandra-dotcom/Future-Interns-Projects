# Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship Projects

This repository contains all three Power BI projects completed during my **Data Science & Analytics Internship at Future Interns**. These projects focus on transforming raw datasets into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, data modeling, visualization, and analytics.

## 📊 Projects Included

### 1. Business Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer orders, and regional trends.

#### Key Features
- Total Sales, Profit, Orders, and Quantity KPIs
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Category-wise Sales & Profit Analysis
- Top Performing Products
- Regional Performance Analysis
- Business Insights & Recommendations

#### Key Insights
- Total Sales: **$2.30M**
- Total Profit: **$286.40K**
- Profit Margin: **12.47%**
- Technology category generated the highest profit
- West region recorded the highest sales
- Canon imageCLASS copier was the top revenue-generating product

---

### 2. Customer Churn & Retention Analysis Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard developed to analyze customer churn behavior, retention performance, and customer engagement patterns.

#### Key Features
- Customer Churn Rate Tracking
- Churn Trend Analysis
- Churn Reason Identification
- Plan-wise Churn Analysis
- Industry-wise Customer Segmentation
- Cohort Retention Analysis

#### Key Insights
- Customer Churn Rate: **22%**
- 110 customers churned out of 500
- Missing features and budget constraints were major churn drivers
- Pro and Basic plans experienced higher churn
- Retention performance varied across customer cohorts

---

### 3. Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard created to evaluate marketing campaign effectiveness and analyze customer conversion behavior.

#### Key Features
- Marketing Funnel Visualization
- Lead-to-Customer Conversion Analysis
- Conversion Channel Comparison
- Customer Segment Analysis
- Education & Occupation-based Insights
- Campaign Performance Evaluation

#### Key Insights
- Total Leads: **41K**
- Total Conversions: **5K**
- Conversion Rate: **11.27%**
- Cellular campaigns outperformed telephone campaigns
- Previous successful interactions increased conversion likelihood
